= Style Demos

== Styling of Lists

* Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit.
* Nulla quis venenatis justo.
* In non _sodales_ eros.

In an admonition it looks like this:

[NOTE]
.Bulleted lists in admonitions
====
* Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit.
* Nulla quis venenatis justo.
* In non _sodales_ eros.
====

But otherwise:

* Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit.
* Nulla quis venenatis justo.
* In non _sodales_ eros.

In block quotes:

____
* Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit.
* Nulla quis venenatis justo.
* In non _sodales_ eros.
____
